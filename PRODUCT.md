# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

B2B buyers at companies that purchase industrial safety supplies: purchasing managers, safety coordinators, and small business owners. They are practical, time-conscious, and need to quickly establish trust in a supplier before making contact. They arrive with a job to do, not to browse.

## Product Purpose

Marketing and lead-generation site for Nilex Industrial & Safety Supplies, a family-run B2B supplier of PPE, lighting, gloves, and industrial facility supplies (founded 2012, woman-owned). Success means a prospect feels confident enough to call or email Mark. The site must establish credibility, communicate product range, and make contact frictionless.

## Brand Personality

Family-run and operated. Approachable professionalism. Authentic, grounded, trustworthy. The tagline "Small Business, Big Safety" captures the voice: a small team that delivers at a level that competes with the big players.

## Anti-references

- Generic big-box industrial catalog feel (Grainger, McMaster-Carr) — cold, itemized, faceless
- Corporate sterile — whitespace deserts, no warmth
- Construction clip-art — hard hats and orange cones as decoration
- Overly slick SaaS — the product is physical goods and real relationships, not software
- Should belong in the industrial/safety market but be distinctive within it

## Design Principles

1. **Market-native, not market-generic** — fit the industry's visual language well enough to be trusted, then differentiate in craft and warmth.
2. **Human behind the business** — the family-run story is the competitive advantage; design should make that feel real, not like a marketing line.
3. **Earned trust** — credibility through clarity and quality, not puffery. Every section should answer "why Nilex?" without needing to say it.
4. **Show capability, signal scale** — the site should feel like a company bigger than it is, without losing the approachable personality.
5. **Contact is the conversion** — every design decision should reduce friction toward picking up the phone or sending an email.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA compliance required. Support reduced motion preferences where animations are present.
